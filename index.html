About XD Magazine

XD Magazine shares captivating Filipino stories that highlight challenges and triumphs in Filipino culture. We aim to inform, entertain, and connect a global audience through powerful narratives.

Mission & Vision

Mission

Our mission is to create a platform that ignites passion through Filipino storytelling and connects a global audience to Filipino experiences.

Vision

We envision XD Magazine as the leading digital platform for Filipino culture, offering a space to explore, enjoy, and share stories that connect us all.

IWAS SAWI PROJECT: Understanding Filipino Emotions

The IWAS SAWI project is a collection of narratives that explores how Filipinos cope with emotions such as heartbreak, failure, and loss. This project aims to bring awareness to mental health and emotional well-being in the Filipino community...

ALAM-AT: The Filipino History Project

ALAM-AT dives deep into the rich and diverse history of the Philippines. From its colonization to its revolutionary movements, this project explores the stories that have shaped Filipino identity...

OUTER SPACE: Exploring Filipino Humor

OUTER SPACE is a comedic exploration of Filipino culture, humor, and the quirky traits that make Filipinos unique. Through satire and light-hearted humor, we uncover the funny side of life in the Philippines...
